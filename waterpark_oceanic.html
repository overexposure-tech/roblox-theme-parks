Waterpark Oceanic

This park was pretty cool.

The theming was... okay. There could've been more plant life.

You also get huge jump abilities... and there are no barriers. So you can really get anywhere on the map.

They offer bathing suites for your character to equip and also... skin colors? Weird choice... but ok.

Almost every button on the UI is a purchase button and when you spawn in you get an immediate portal ad. But this one gets a plus because there's a button to report bugs.

The rest of this will be split up into the parks different areas.

Ice World

Next to the actual Cave Crawler tube, there is this odd tube where there is no station, and it leads from one part of the Jungle to another. I don't know what this is.

Rides/Attractions:
  • Cave Crawler (Ride) - 6 Person Tube Ride
  • Stone Way (Ride) - 2 Person Tube Ride
  • Ice Spouts (Ride) - 2 Person Tube Ride
  • Toboggan Racers (Ride) - Like Those Fair Slides
  • Arctic Drop (Ride) - 2 Person Water Drop Ride

Jungle

My favorite ride from this area was Tarzan and Jane. In this area, there's a parkour area which would definitely not be allowed in real life (Take that how you will).

There are only 2 rides, but 3 attractions.

Rides/Attractions:
  • Jungle Run (Parkour)
  • Tarzan & Jane (Ride) - Two Person Double Flume Ride
  • Volcano (Ride) - 6 Person Tube Ride

Fey's Adventure

This area is literally just a big water slide. My character hit the side of the tube and came to a stop, so I didn't get to enjoy the full ride.
